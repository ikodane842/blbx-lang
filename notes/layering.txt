 - the Parsing layer will contain parsing for scopes (SCOPE) denoted by curly brackets
 this will be used for object, class, and function declaration 
    FUNCTION_SCOPE, OBJECT_SCOPE, CLASS_SCOPE

 - the IR layer will contain parsing for specific branching function calls, 
 loop functtion calls and regular function calls
    BRANCH & FUNCTION_CALL & FOR_LOOP & WHILE_LOOP

 - the IR layer will handle indexing 
 `(array[2]).concat(", sevenths")`

 - the IR layer will handle abstracted classes 
 like singletons and Loop Nodes (`__`)
